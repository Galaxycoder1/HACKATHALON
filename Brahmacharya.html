What The Vedas Teaches Us About Brahmacharya

*The state of celibate student life : the initial stage of the Brahmanic ashramas


*Brahmacharya, (Sanskrit: “pure conduct”) in Buddhism, strictly, the practice of chastity. More generally, the term denotes the endeavour by monks and nuns as well as lay devotees to live a moral life as a way to end suffering and to reach enlightenment
